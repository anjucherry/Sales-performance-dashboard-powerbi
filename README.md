# Sales Performance Dashboard

An interactive Power BI dashboard project built using AdventureWorks sales data. The project includes comprehensive Sales Performance, Regional Analysis, Product Analysis, and Customer Insights dashboards. It leverages DAX, data modeling, Power Query, maps, slicers, and drill-through analysis for deep business insights.

## Project Overview

This project transforms raw AdventureWorks sales datasets into structured, meaningful business insights. The focus is on key areas like profitability, regional trends, product success, and customer behavior.

The dashboard suite provides insights into:

*   Total and Net Sales performance vs. targets.
*   Regional and country-level sales trends and territory analysis.
*   Profitability and product category performance.
*   Customer segmentation and demographics.
*   Key customer and order details via drill-through.

***

## Dashboard Pages

### 1. Sales Performance & Regional Analysis

This is the primary dashboard page, focusing on the high-level health of the business and geographical sales distribution. It shows key metrics and trends.

**Key Insights:**

*   **KPIs:** Total Sales vs. Target, Gross Profit, and Net Sales.
*   **Performance Trends:** Monthly sales comparisons and performance against goals.
*   **Geographic View:** Interative maps and charts detailing performance by region and country.
*   **Segmentation:** Sales breakdowns by major categories (Accessories, Bikes, Clothing).

### 2. Product Analysis

This page provides a deep dive into product category and individual product performance, helping to identify top sellers and opportunities.

**Key Insights:**

*   **Product Performance Matrix:** Comparative analysis of sales volume, margin, and order quantity across product lines.
*   **Profitability View:** Detailed breakdowns of gross profit and margin by product and category.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Understanding product category performance over time.

<img src="Product Analysis_Dashboard.PNG" alt="Product Analysis Dashboard Page" width="100%">

### 3. Customer Insights

This dashboard focuses on understanding the customer base, demographics, and key customer behavior, which can inform targeted marketing.

**Key Insights:**

*   **Customer Demographics:** Breakdowns by marital status, occupation, and total orders.
*   **Profit Analysis:** Understanding profitability relative to customer groups.
*   **Order Details:** Key customer and order information.
*   **Drill-through:** A detailed drill-through view is available for specific orders, showing granular details of order dates, shipping, and product breakdowns.
